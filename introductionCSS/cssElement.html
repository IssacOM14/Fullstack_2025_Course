CSS stands for Cascading Style Sheets, a cascade is pretty much a waterfall, we have a level and drops down to another level. The style starts from the most general all the way down to the most specific style requirements. The Style Sheet means a type of language similar to HTML, the SS is a language to specify how things should look in our website. We also have Sass or Less. The most important one is CSS.
